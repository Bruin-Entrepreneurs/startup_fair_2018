About

StartupFair LA is an event that aims to connect the best of UCLA with the best of the LA Startup Scene. It's a great way for those with interest in entrepreneurship to learn more about what it's like to work at a startup and find Full time or Internship positions.

StartupFair LA also provides a unique opportunity to startups in the LA Area by giving them a chance to interact with UCLA's best and brightest and spread the word about their exciting work. This is one of the few startup-exclusive events at UCLA, so take advantage of this chance to recruit some of the best talent in the area!
